## ?? NLP Implementation Details

### Text Featurization
- **Method**: ML.NET's FeaturizeText transformer
- **Algorithm**: TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency)
- **Output**: 50-dimensional numerical vectors
- **Processing**: Both students and queries vectorized identically

### Similarity Matching
- **Formula**: Cosine Similarity = (V? · V?) / (||V?|| × ||V?||)
- **Range**: 0 (no similarity) to 1 (perfect match)
- **Advantage**: Fast, efficient, semantically meaningful

### Why No Azure Cognitive Search?
? Pure ML.NET implementation
? No cloud dependencies
? Lightweight and fast
? Educational value (understand NLP internals)
? Cost-effective for small datasets
? Full control over algorithm
